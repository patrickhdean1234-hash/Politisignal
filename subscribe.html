Common Questions

When will I be charged?
You're charged immediately when you subscribe. Your billing cycle starts from that date and renews monthly.
What does "founding price locked forever" mean?
As PolitiSignal grows, we'll raise prices. But if you join now, your price is frozen at today's rate for as long as you keep your subscription active. Cancel and resubscribe, and you'll pay the new rate.
Is this financial advice?
No. PolitiSignal provides information and analysis for educational purposes only. Nothing here constitutes investment advice. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.
How does the API access work (Elite)?
After subscribing, hit GET /api/signals from any language. Filter by severity, ticker, or politician. Updated every 5 minutes. No auth token required — access is on the honor system while we build full auth.
How do push notifications work?
On the dashboard, click "Enable Push" in the sidebar. Your browser will ask for permission. After that, you'll receive real-time alerts for critical and high-severity signals — even when the tab is in the background.
How do I cancel?
Manage or cancel at billing.stripe.com — no email needed, instant self-serve. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
